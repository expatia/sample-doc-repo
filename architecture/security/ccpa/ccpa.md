# California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

The California Consumer Privacy Act, abbreviated CCPA, is a California state statute intended to enhance privacy rights and consumer protection for residents of teh sate of California in the United States. This is often adopted by companies and organizations outside of the California as best practice and in order to future-proof regulatory compliance in markets beyond those of California itself.

This document and other documents in this section of the documentation sets out Compass data protection policies and how, if at all, these align with CCPA.
