# YouTube Channels

This section describes how use our YouTube channel to help achieve the goals set out in the [Content](./README.md) section of this document set.

We publish videos on our YouTube channel to:

* advertize our products
* educate users about our products
* engage with our users to build brand loyalty
* engage with our users to build a community around our products and services


## Relevance to Software Architecture

We build our software to integrate with our various content channels. We use our content channels to drive sales prospects into our software so we can:

* educate sales prospects about the value of our software
* demonstrate our software products quickly, cheaply but more importantly effectively
* convert sales prospects into paying customers
* retain existing customers by educating them about the new features of our software products and building a greater appreciation of existing features.
