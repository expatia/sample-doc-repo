# Digital Marketing Strategy

This document describes what our Digital Marketing Strategy is, the channels that we use and how we integrate these with our software products and services.

## Overview

An overview of our digital marketing strategy will appear here. Our software products will be built from the ground up to facilitate powerful digital marketing techniques and features.

## Components

The following components are incorporated into our Digital Market Strategy.

| Component | Description |
| --------- | ----------- |
| [Blogs](./blogs.md) | SEO-friendly promotion of our products and services with free tutorials about how to get the most our of our product range and why it's superior to offerings from our competitors. |
| [BlueSky](./bluesky.md) | Community engagement around our brand. This is used to attract, involve and retain existing and potential customers and clients. |
| [LinkedIn](./linkedin.md) | Targeting of strategic business partners, collaborators and enterprise customers who are likely to generate business. |
| [YouTube](./youtube.md) | YouTube channels are used for demonstrations of our products and also to build communities around our products and services. |
